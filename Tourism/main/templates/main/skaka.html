The city of Sakaka is located in the heart of the Saudi Al-Jawf region, and is administratively subject to Prince Fahd bin Badr bin Abdulaziz Al Saud. Geographically, it is approximately 566 meters above sea level, and occupies a space in the northern part of the country. The city of Sakaka is affected by the continental desert climate. Its winters are cold and its summers are very hot. The maximum temperature in summer reaches more than 42 degrees Celsius, while temperatures drop in winter to 7 degrees below zero.
